General rules & reminders
  • Agricultural-to-agricultural conversions for personal farming generally attract no conversion fee across most Indian states.
  • Official legal units in India are Hectare and Square Meter (since 1957), but traditional local units remain in active use in revenue and registration records.
  • The same unit name (e.g. Bigha, Katha) has different sizes in different states — always verify the local multiplier before any transaction.
  • Land conversion permission must be obtained from the competent revenue authority before changing land use. Fees vary by state government circulars and are revised periodically.
  • For accurate stamp duty and registration charges, consult your state's sub-registrar office or official e-registration portal.
